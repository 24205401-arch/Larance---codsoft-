Core Skills
Everything You'll Learn to Master
🏗️

Sections & Columns

Learn to divide your page into logical, well-structured sections and create multi-column layouts that look great on any screen.

🎨

Color Palette Mastery

Discover how to choose cohesive color schemes that convey emotion, guide user attention, and make designs visually stunning.

📐

Alignment & Padding

Master the art of spacing — padding, margin, and alignment — to create breathing room and visual hierarchy in every layout.

🧱

Boxes & the Box Model

Understand how CSS treats every element as a box. Learn borders, padding, margin, and how to avoid overlapping elements.

🔤

Headers & Footers

Build professional navigation bars and footers that anchor your design, improve usability, and establish brand identity.

💡

Creative Design Thinking

Unleash your creativity — learn to make design decisions that are not just functional but genuinely impressive and memorable.

Process
How the Learning Journey Works
01

Learn the Basics

Start with HTML structure and fundamental CSS properties. Understand tags, selectors, and the cascade.

02

Plan Your Layout

Sketch sections, choose your color palette, and plan the visual hierarchy before writing a single line of code.

03

Build & Style

Implement your design using HTML and CSS. Apply Flexbox, Grid, padding, and colors mindfully.

04

Polish & Submit

Review alignments, fix overlapping elements, make it responsive, and submit a polished, professional result.
